Weightroom and Fitness Studio

Southland Leisure Center

Southland Leisure Centre features a 7,000-square-foot weight room and 4,300-square-foot fitness studio located on the second level.

Weight room features

- free weights

- plate loaded machines

- treadmills

- steppers

- stepmills

- cross-trainers

- rowing machines

- recumbent and upright bikes

- adjustable pulley systems

- synergy 360T multi-station training apparatus

- a variety of selectorized equipment with the latest in biangular action

- wheelchair accessible equipment, Equalizer strength machine and Technogym hand ergometer

The weight room has a distributed music system that features satellite radio that can be used in various areas of the weight room. The cardio area hosts Broadcast Vision with four televisions each attached to a FM receiver, which can be tuned in on a personal stereo to listen to the television of your choice.

Orientations are offered to all new and existing pass holders (minimum 6 month pass) at no charge. Please pre-book your 30 minute orientation with a weight room monitor or call 403-648-6572
